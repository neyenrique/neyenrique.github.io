Contrasting Romania

In front of the Black Sea in Romania, people live very contrasting realities. Tourists enjoy their vacation while the Roma community lives in a dump after being evicted by local authorities. Romas sell their mod at the beach during the summer to the rich tourists that come from all over Europe.
