About

Ney Alvarez has been working in media as a multimedia producer and journalist since 1993. He has worked in different media outlets like magazines, newspapers, radio, television, public affairs, and as a political campaigns advisor. Ney is also an expert in consistently entertaining large audiences with tireless enthusiasm on his radio show or hosting public events.

Ney is currently working on the production of his documentary "Got Prep?" as part of his thesis project for a Masters in Multimedia Journalism. Here you will find part of the work he has done over the years.
